### END commands:

```
G92 E0
G0 Z130 F300; Go high

G0 X77.7 Y52.3 F3000
G92 X0 Y0 ; Change Offset back to center

G0 X0 Y50 F3000; Go front and center
M104 S0 ; Turn off temperature
M107 ; Turn on fan
M84 ; Disable motors
```
